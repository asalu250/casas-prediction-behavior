Privacy & data

What's tracked
Motion, door, and item sensors around your home — never audio, video, or location outside it.
Where it's processed
The status you see is computed from your own sensor history. Your companion never receives raw sensor data — only the plain-language summary shown in the preview.
What a companion sees
A short email, only when you've turned sharing on — never timestamps or room-by-room detail.
Your data, your control
Turn off sharing at any time — it stops the next scheduled email immediately. Every settings change is recorded with a timestamp.
This app notices patterns in daily routine — it does not diagnose, and a flagged day is not a medical finding. If something feels wrong, please talk to a person, not just this screen.
